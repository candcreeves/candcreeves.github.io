Eugene

The Gordon Hotel 555 Oak St, Eugene, OR 97401

Set in the Market District of Eugene -- a quiet area with dining & shopping at 5th Street Public Market & city views from Skinner Butte Park -- this trendy hotel is a 2-minute walk from the Hult Center for Performing Arts and 9 miles from Eugene Airport. Thereâ€™s a complimentary airport shuttle. Other amenities include loaner bikes, a lobby bar and a 24-hour fitness center.

Hyatt Place Eugene 333 Oakway Rd, Eugene, OR 97401

Featuring mountain views, this casual hotel in a shopping center off Interstate 105 is a mile from fishing and trails at 373-acre Alton Baker Park, and 2 miles from the University of Oregon. Some quarters feature city or mountain views. Freebies include parking, breakfast and local area shuttle service. An on-site market/eatery is open 24/7, and includes Starbucks coffee. Other amenities consist of a bar with a rooftop terrace, plus an indoor pool, a hot tub and a gym.

Springfield

Holiday Inn Express 919 Kruse Way, Springfield, OR 97477

This contemporary hotel off I-5 is a 7-minute drive from Eugene and a .9-mile drive from Sacred Heart Medical Center at RiverBend. Kids 19 and under stay free with an adult. Amenities include an on-site Italian restaurant (where kids 12 and under eat free), heated indoor pool, fitness center and event facilities, plus free parking and airport shuttle service.

La Quinta Inn & Suites 3480 Hutton St, Springfield, OR 97477

In a commercial plaza off Interstate 5, this informal hotel is a mile from both PeaceHealth Sacred Heart Medical Center and the Shoppes at Gateway mall. It's 6 miles from the University of Oregon. Amenities include an indoor pool and a 24-hour gym, as well as a convenience store and a coin-operated laundry. A breakfast buffet and parking are available.
